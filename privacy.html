## Privacy Policy ### Introduction The Jira Tab Checker extension respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data. ### Information We Collect The extension reads the Jira ticket ID and status from open tabs. No data is stored, shared, or transmitted to external servers. ### How We Use Your Information All data is processed locally in your browser and is not collected or shared with any third party. ### Permissions The extension requires access to browser tabs to check Jira ticket statuses. It does not modify or store any information. ### Contact If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@example.com.
