Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Jira Tab Checker extension handles your data.

Information We Collect

The extension reads the Jira ticket ID and status from the currently open tabs. No data is stored, shared, or transmitted to external servers.

How We Use Your Information

All data is processed locally on your browser and is not collected or shared with any third party.

Permissions

The extension requires permission to access browser tabs to check Jira ticket statuses.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@example.com.
